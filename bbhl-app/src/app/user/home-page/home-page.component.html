Try my Draft Simulator!

Pick your BBHL franchise, randomize a draft order, and start picking. This fully functional simulator includes a dashboard to help you sort through the available players and visualize your lineup as you draft.

Play some BBHLdoku

Think you know everyone in league? Prove it in this PuckDoku inspired guessing game. Name players who've bounced around the league and get rewarded for more obscure guesses.

Statistics & Results

Dive into the BBHL archives and see player stats and standings from years past. Check out game results and boxscores from last season, or put on your pretend admin hat and generate your own results and boxscores from the Admin hub.
